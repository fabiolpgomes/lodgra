## 📝 Dev Notes

### Backlink Strategy

**High-Authority Targets (DA > 40):**
- Industry publications (vacation rental blogs)
- Business directories (property management associations)
- Tourism websites (Portugal/Brazil/Spain travel blogs)
- Educational institutions (university resources on property management)

**Guest Posting Topics:**
- "Tips for Property Owners" (properties)
- "Vacation Rental Success Stories" (case studies)
- "Property Management Best Practices" (guides)

### GMB Optimization

- Ensure all business categories selected
- Add business description (keyword-optimized)
- Upload 10+ property/team/office photos
- Schedule 1 post per week (property tips, updates)
- Monitor reviews (respond to all within 24h)

### Local Citations

**Key Directories:**
- Google My Business (primary)
- Yelp (vacation rental category)
- TripAdvisor (property listings)
- Airbnb (partner link, if applicable)
- Tourism bureau websites (PT, BR, ES)

### Monitoring Setup

- Use Google Search Console for authority signals
- Track backlinks (manual list or tool: Ahrefs/SEMrush)
- Monitor GMB insights (actions, direction requests, calls)
- Track local rankings (Google Map results for target keywords)
