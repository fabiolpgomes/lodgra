# Story 31.5: Onboarding com Organização e Link de Reserva Direta

**Epic:** Epic 31 — Multi-Tenant Infrastructure (Branded Direct Booking)  
**Status:** Ready for Review  
**Priority:** P0  
**Type:** Product + Frontend + Backend  
**Assigned to:** @dev

## Story

**As** a new Lodgra customer,  
**I want** to define my organization name and branded booking subdomain during onboarding,  
**So that** I can see and share my direct booking page immediately after setup.

## Context

Epic 31 is already marked Done for multi-tenant branded direct booking. The documented architecture uses `organizations.slug` and subdomain routing to serve company booking pages at `nomeempresa.lodgra.io/booking`.

The current onboarding still starts with plan selection, but the post-checkout flow already has a selected paid plan. The first useful setup step should be organization identity and direct booking URL.
