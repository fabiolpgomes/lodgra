- [ ] Article: "Como Rentabilizar Seu Imóvel em Portugal & Brasil"

- [ ] Primary keyword: "rentabilizar imóvel" (integrated naturally, ≤2% density)
- [ ] 3-4 secondary keywords integrated (natural placement, no stuffing)
- [ ] **Success Metric:** Article indexed in Google within 7 days
- [ ] CTA implemented (newsletter signup OR property inquiry)

- [ ] Meta title optimized (keyword-first, 50-60 chars)
- [ ] Meta description with CTA (150-160 chars)
- [ ] H1 = article title (includes primary keyword)
- [ ] **Success Metric:** 3-5 H2 sections with secondary keywords
- [ ] Images with descriptive alt text (3-5 images minimum)
- [ ] Schema: Article + Author markup applied

### AC5: Multilingual Content Setup
- [ ] Portuguese (PT-BR) version published
- [ ] Spanish (ES) version content outline created
- [ ] **Success Metric:** hreflang implementation verified
- [ ] Language switcher functional (PT/BR, ES, EN routes)

### Article Structure

**Markdown Format:**
```markdown
---
title: "Como Rentabilizar Seu Imóvel em Portugal & Brasil"
description: "Guia completo..."
author: "Lodgra Team"
date: "2026-05-17"
readTime: "8 min"
keyword: "rentabilizar imóvel"
---

# Como Rentabilizar Seu Imóvel... [H1]

Intro paragraph with keyword...

## Seção 1: ... [H2 with keyword]
Content...

## Seção 2: ... [H2]
Content with internal links...

## CTA Section
Newsletter signup or property inquiry form
```

### Localization (i18n)

- Use Next.js i18n routing: `/pt/blog/`, `/es/blog/`, `/en/blog/`
- Implement hreflang tags: `<link rel="alternate" hreflang="es" href="..." />`
- Language switcher in header/footer
